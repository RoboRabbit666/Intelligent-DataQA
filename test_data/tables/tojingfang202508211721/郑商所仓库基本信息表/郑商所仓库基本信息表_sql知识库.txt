问题：统计各省级区域的总交割库容是多少？

问题：哪些省份的仓库数量多，但平均交割库容较小？
SELECT PRVC, COUNT(*) as warehouse_count, AVG(DLV_WH_CPY) as avg_dlv_capacity
FROM dataqa.wh_base_info_tab
where stas_dt='2025-07-02'
GROUP BY PRVC
ORDER BY warehouse_count DESC, avg_dlv_capacity ASC;

问题：分析不同仓库性质的交割库容分布情况
SELECT WH_OWN, 
       MIN(DLV_WH_CPY) as min_capacity,
       AVG(DLV_WH_CPY) as avg_capacity,

       SUM(DLV_WH_CPY) as total_capacity
FROM dataqa.wh_base_info_tab

GROUP BY WH_OWN;
